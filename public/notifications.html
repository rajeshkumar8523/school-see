School Notifications

01

Important Announcements

Notifications for school-wide announcements, including changes to schedules, policy updates,

or important reminders, ensure that parents and students are always informed about critical information.

02

Event Reminders

Parents receive reminders about upcoming events such as parent-teacher conferences, school holidays,

sports events, and extracurricular activities. This helps families plan ahead and participate in school activities.

03

Emergency Alerts

In case of emergencies, such as school closures due to weather conditions or safety concerns,

notifications provide immediate updates to ensure the safety and well-being of students and staff.

04

Attendance Alerts

Notifications about attendance issues, such as absences or tardiness, help keep parents informed about

their child's attendance status and encourage timely communication between parents and teachers.

05

Academic Performance Updates

Parents receive notifications about significant academic updates, such as exam schedules, report card releases,

and important assessments, keeping them engaged in their child’s education.

06

Health and Safety Updates

Notifications about health guidelines, vaccination drives, or wellness programs help keep parents informed

about the measures being taken for the safety of students.

07

Extracurricular Activity Updates

Information about clubs, sports teams, and other extracurricular activities keeps parents informed

about opportunities for their children to get involved outside of academics.

08

Timely Information

Notifications ensure that parents and students receive timely information,

allowing them to make informed decisions and preparations.
