FAQ

1. What is the purpose of the app?

The app helps track and manage student attendance digitally, reducing paperwork and improving accuracy.

2. How do I log in?

Use your registered email/phone number and the provided password or OTP.

3. What if I forget my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" option to reset it via email or SMS.

4. How is attendance marked?

Teachers can manually mark attendance or use automated methods like QR codes or biometrics (if supported).

5. Can parents view attendance records?

Yes, parents can access their child’s attendance history through their linked accounts.

6. How do I update student details?

Navigate to the “Profile” or “Student Details” section to make updates.

7. What reports are available?

Attendance trends, daily/weekly/monthly reports, and alerts for low attendance are provided.

8. How do notifications work?

The app sends real-time alerts for absences, attendance status, or any related updates.

9. Is data secure?

Yes, the app uses encryption and secure login protocols to protect data.

10. Who to contact for support?

Use the “Help” or “Contact Us” section in the app for assistance.
