Bus Tracking

01

Tracking System

Utilizes GPS technology to monitor the location of school buses in real time.

Parents can access this information through a user-friendly app or website, enabling you to see exactly where the bus is .

02

Real-Time Location Tracking

Track the bus's location in real time, allowing you to see when it will arrive at your

designated stop. This eliminates uncertainty and helps you plan your schedule accordingly.

03

Arrival Notifications

Receive instant notifications when the bus is approaching your stop. This feature ensures that you’re always informed,

reducing wait times and ensuring your child is ready to board.

04

Route History

Access past routes taken by the bus. This transparency helps you

understand the bus’s schedule and any changes that may occur.

05

Safety Alerts

Get alerts for any unexpected delays, detours, or incidents,

keeping you informed about your child’s journey and safety.

06

User-Friendly Interface

Our app is designed with parents in mind, featuring an intuitive

layout that makes tracking simple and efficient. You can easily view information on the go.

07

Enhanced Safety

Real-time tracking and immediate notifications mean you can

respond quickly in case of any delays or issues.

08

Improved Time Management

With accurate arrival times and notifications, you can better

manage your schedule and ensure your child is safely at the bus stop when it arrives.
