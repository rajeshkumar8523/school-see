Fee Status

01

Real-Time Updates

Get instant information on your current fee balance, upcoming payments, and due dates.

Easily download or print receipts and invoices for your records or reimbursements.

02

Total Fee Overview

The system displays the total fee amount for each student, providing a clear

understanding of the overall financial commitment for the academic year or term.

03

Paid Amount

Parents can view the total amount that has been paid so far. This section helps in tracking payments

made throughout the academic year and ensures that parents are aware of their current financial standing.

04

Due Amount

The fee status feature clearly indicates any outstanding amounts that are still due.

This helps parents understand what they owe and ensures timely payments to avoid any late fees or penalties.

05

Payment History

Users can access a detailed history of all payments made, including dates and amounts.

This feature provides transparency and helps parents keep track of their payment history.

06

Payment Deadlines and Alerts

The system can send notifications about upcoming payment deadlines, reminding parents when fees are due.

This proactive approach helps avoid missed payments and associated complications.

07

Detailed Fee Breakdown

Parents can view a breakdown of the fees, including tuition, extracurricular activities, transportation,

and any other charges. This transparency helps parents understand exactly what they are paying for.

08

Support for Budgeting

By providing a clear picture of the total fees and payment schedules,

parents can better budget for school expenses, helping to alleviate financial stress.
