Attendence Graph

01

Visual Representation of Attendance Data

The attendance graph displays a clear, visual representation of each student’s attendance over a specified period

(daily, weekly, monthly). This visual format makes it easy to spot trends and identify patterns at a glance.

02

Detailed Breakdown

The graph includes details on present, absent, and late days, providing a holistic view of attendance.

This breakdown helps in understanding the reasons behind absences or lateness.

03

Customizable Time Frames

Users can select specific time frames to view attendance data. This feature allows parents and teachers to monitor

attendance during particular terms, months, or weeks, making it easier to assess changes over time.

04

Comparison Tools

Compare a student’s attendance against class averages or school-wide

attendance rates, offering context for individual performance and engagement levels.

05

Alerts for Attendance Issues

The system can generate alerts when a student’s

attendance falls below a certain threshold, helping parents and teachers address potential issues early.

06

Improved Accountability

Parents can easily monitor their child’s attendance,

fostering accountability and encouraging students to prioritize regular school attendance.

07

Data-Driven Decisions

Teachers and administrators can use attendance data to make informed decisions about student support,

identifying those who may need additional help or encouragement to attend school regularly.

08

Enhanced Communication

The graph serves as a tool for meaningful conversations between parents and students regarding attendance patterns,

promoting discussions about the importance of being present and engaged in school.
